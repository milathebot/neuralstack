## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

NeuralStack is a functional snack brand launching in Toronto, Ontario. We make nootropics-first gummy snacks for gamers, coders, and desk workers — with a protein line coming in Phase 2 for gym bros.

We're starting small, validated, and event-first. Budget: $5–10K. Timeline: 12 weeks to first sale.

**Positioning:** Functional fuel for focused minds and performing bodies. Not another supplement brand. Not another protein bar. A snack brand that actually tastes good and actually works.

**Tagline:** Stack your cognitive and physical gains.

**Price:** $6 wholesale / $9.99–$12.99 retail (30-serving pouches)

**Audience:** 18–40, gamers/coders/desk workers (nootropics) | Gym bros 21–40 (protein)

**Channel:** Events first — Superteam Canada, Solana events, festivals, markets. Own the circuit.

## 9. REGULATORY

### Canada Requirements

**No NPN required** for conventional functional snacks with structure/function claims (not drug claims).

**Allowed claims:**
- "Contains lion's mane for focus"
- "Vitamin B6 contributes to normal cognitive function"
- "Source of protein"
- "Natural source of antioxidants"

**Forbidden claims:**
- "Treats [condition]"
- "Cures [condition]"
- "Clinically proven" (requires RCT)
- Any enzyme/metabolic claim without evidence

**Label requirements:**
- Bilingual (English + French)
- Nutrition Facts table
- Allergen declarations (top 10)
- Ingredient list (descending order by weight)
- Net quantity (metric)
- Manufacturer name + address

**Lion's mane / reishi:** Accepted as food ingredients in Canada. Not novel. Safe to use.

**Budget for regulatory review:** $1,000–$1,500 before packaging print.
