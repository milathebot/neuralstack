# Overclock — Label Artwork Spec
**Version 1 | May 2026**

This brief tells any designer exactly what to build. If you're doing it yourself, follow this spec exactly.

---

## LABEL OVERVIEW

You need TWO things for launch:
1. **Primary label** — wraps around the tin (the main label)
2. **Back label** — Ingredient list + nutrition facts (CFIA required)

---

## PRIMARY LABEL SPEC

**Dimensions:**
- Width: 140mm (fits 40–45mm diameter tins when wrapped)
- Height: 50mm (standard tin label height)
- Bleed: +3mm on all sides (146mm × 56mm with bleed)
- Resolution: 300 DPI minimum

**File format:** PDF (print-ready) or PNG at 300 DPI

---

## DESIGN — FRONT

**Background:** Solid #0D0D0D (near-black) — or very dark espresso brown #1A1208

**Typography:**
- Brand name: "OVERCLOCK" — bold, clean sans-serif (use Inter, Helvetica Neue, or similar)
  - Size: 18pt minimum, tracked out +20 to +40
- Product name: "FOCUS MINTS" — regular weight, 10pt
  - Below brand name, tight tracking
- Flavor descriptor: "chocolate · espresso" — 8pt, uppercase, wide tracking (+60 to +80), muted color #8B7355
- Net weight: "30 tablets" — 7pt, bottom of label, muted

**Color palette on label:**
- Background: #0D0D0D
- Brand name: #D4A853 (warm amber — "OVERCLOCK")
- Descriptor text: #8B7355 (espresso brown — muted)
- Accent line: thin horizontal rule in #D4A853, 0.5pt weight

**Visual elements:**
- 1 horizontal accent line dividing brand from descriptor
- Optional: small leaf/shroom motif (very subtle, 5–8mm max, #2A1F14 color) — suggests functional ingredients without making health claims
- No photos, no illustrations, no gradients

**DO NOT include:**
- Any health claims ("focus," "energy," "cognition," "nootropic," "natural")
- Any ingredient percentages or active amounts
- Any barcode on the front (back label only)

---

## BACK LABEL SPEC

**Dimensions:** Same as front — 140mm × 50mm

**Background:** White #FFFFFF (clean, legible)

**Layout — top to bottom:**
1. Brand name + product name (small, top)
2. Ingredient list (descending order, bilingual if possible — English first)
3. Net weight + batch info
4. Nutrition Facts table (CFIA format)
5. Business name + location (bottom)

**Ingredient list format:**
```
Ingredients: Xylitol, mannitol, Dutch-processed cocoa powder, coffee extract, ceremonial matcha powder, lion's mane (Hericium erinaceus) dual-extract, vanilla powder, natural chocolate flavor, sea salt, stevia reb M, calcium stearate.

Contains: Xylitol. Excessive consumption may cause gastrointestinal discomfort.
```

**Nutrition Facts (CFIA format):**
```
Nutrition Facts
Per 1 tablet (1g)
                % Daily Value*
Calories              0
Fat                   0g    0%
Sodium                0mg   0%
Total Carbohydrate     1g    0%
  Includes 0g Added Sugars
Sugar Alcohol          1g
Protein                0g
*5% or less is a little, 15% or more is a lot
```

**Bottom of back label:**
```
Overclock Foods — Toronto, Ontario, Canada
Product of Canada
```

## BILINGUAL REQUIREMENTS (CFIA)

Labels must be in both English and French. For v1, English is acceptable if sold only online with no physical retail presence in Quebec. For retail in Quebec, bilingual is mandatory.

**Minimum bilingual elements:**
- Product name
- Ingredient list
- Net weight
- Business name and address
- "Product of Canada" / "Produit du Canada"

**French translations for common ingredients:**
- Xylitol → Xylitol
- Mannitol → Mannitol
- Dutch-processed cocoa powder → Poudre de cacao processed hollandaise
- Coffee extract → Extrait de cafe
- Matcha powder → Poudre de matcha
- Lion's mane → Crinière de lion
- Vanilla powder → Poudre de vanille
- Natural chocolate flavor → Arôme naturel de chocolat
- Sea salt → Sel marin
- Stevia → Stévia
- Calcium stearate → Stéarate de calcium
- Sugar-free → Sans sucre
- Vegan → Végane
- Gluten-free → Sans gluten

## LABEL MOCKUP DESCRIPTION (for designer)

"For a functional mint product called Overclock. The brand aesthetic is dark espresso/cacao — like a specialty coffee roaster, not a wellness brand. Think Ledger/Arc/Linear software aesthetic energy.

Front label: Near-black background, warm amber brand name 'OVERCLOCK', minimal and premium. A thin horizontal line. 'FOCUS MINTS' in smaller text below. 'chocolate · espresso' in muted espresso brown, widely tracked. No illustrations, no photos. Just type and one thin line.

Back label: Clean white, standard food label layout. Ingredient list in small type. CFIA nutrition facts table. Bottom: 'Overclock Foods — Toronto, Ontario, Canada.'

The tin itself is a flip-top metal tin — silver or matte black. The label wraps around the body of the tin."

## PRINT SPECS

**Material:** Glossy vinyl, laminate finish (water-resistant, food-safe)

**Proofing:** Order 1 proof before full run. Sticker Mule offers free proofs.

**Turnaround:** 5–7 business days to Toronto
