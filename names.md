# Brand Name Options — Functional Snack Brand
*15 options, 3 tiers, Toronto launch*

---

## TIER 1 — Names I Love

---

**1. NeuralStack**
- **Tagline:** "Stack your cognitive and physical gains."
- **Rationale:** "Neural" hits the nootropic/brain angle. "Stack" is gamer vernacular for combining supplements AND gym parlance for progressive overload. One word, punchy, works for both audiences. Implies layering benefits. Domain likely available as NeuralStack.co or .ca.
- **Domain risk:** Medium — "NeuralStack" supplement brand exists in US but not Canada. Recommend NeuralStack.ca with early registration.

---

**2. Synapse**
- **Tagline:** "Firing on all neurons and muscle fibers."
- **Rationale:** Scientific but sleek. Speaks to synaptic plasticity (brain health) while hinting at muscle fiber recruitment. Premium, memorable, and works without explanation. Short. Strong visual identity potential (the synapse symbol).
- **Domain risk:** Low — Synapse.ca likely available. "Synapse Labs" exists in US but not Canada.

---

**3. Forge**
- **Tagline:** "Fuel for minds that build and bodies that perform."
- **Rationale:** Evocative of creation (coders/gamers building worlds) and physical transformation (gym). Short, strong, premium feel. No literal food/snack connotation — leaves room for brand story. Canadian forestry/industrial heritage resonates locally.
- **Domain risk:** Low — Forge.co and Forge.ca likely available. No major food brand owns it in Canada.

---

**4. Shift**
- **Tagline:** "The mental shift. The physical shift. Your daily edge."
- **Rationale:** "Shift" is the gamer's most-used key and gym language for progressive overload. Ultra-short, modern, punchy. French-friendly — "Shift" reads the same in both languages. Excellent for bilingual Canadian market.
- **Domain risk:** Very low — Shift.ca probably available. Not owned by any food brand.

---

**5. Clutch**
- **Tagline:** "When it counts — cognitively and physically."
- **Rationale:** Gaming term for high-pressure plays + gym slang for grinding out final reps. Premium-feeling, aspirational, universally understood by the target demo. Implies reliability and performance under pressure.
- **Domain risk:** Low — Clutch.ca likely available. Clutch Brands exists but for different categories.

---

## TIER 2 — Solid Options

---

**6. Lucid**
- **Tagline:** "Clarity in every crunch."
- **Rationale:** Clean, premium, suggests mental clarity (nootropics) with a physical product hint. French-friendly (*Lucide* reads well). Elegant but might skew more nootropic than gym. Good for event sampling — memorable.
- **Domain risk:** Low — Lucid.ca likely available.

---

**7. Peak**
- **Tagline:** "Cognition at peak. Performance at peak."
- **Rationale:** Clean, aspirational, works for both audiences. Classic fitness word elevated by nootropic positioning. Generic enough to build on but specific enough to own. Domain probably premium-priced but doable.
- **Domain risk:** Medium — Peak Performance brands exist; need Peak.co or Peak.ca specifically.

---

**8. Ampl**
- **Tagline:** "Your performance, amplified."
- **Rationale:** Tech-startup brevity meets gym supplement energy. Short, memorable, very web-native. Drawback: slightly generic for a snack brand — needs strong visual identity to own it. Ampl.ca likely available.
- **Domain risk:** Low — Ampl.ca likely free.

---

**9. Momentum**
- **Tagline:** "Build it cognitively. Build it physically. Don't stop."
- **Rationale:** Speaks to the grind mentality of gym bros and the persistent debugging mindset of coders. Universally relatable but less distinctive — more of an adjective than a brand name. Solid backup.
- **Domain risk:** Medium — Momentum health brands exist; need to check Momentum.ca specifically.

---

**10. Grid**
- **Tagline:** "Where neurons connect and muscle fibers fire."
- **Rationale:** Gaming grid (esports/computing) + muscle fiber grid. Abstract, cool, premium. Risk: too conceptual for a snack brand — needs heavy brand investment to own it. Wildcard that could become iconic or fall flat.
- **Domain risk:** Low — Grid.ca likely available.

---

## TIER 3 — Wildcards

---

**11. Respawn**
- **Tagline:** "Reboot. Refuel. Return stronger."
- **Rationale:** Pure gaming DNA — "respawn" is the recovery mechanic. Gym bros also understand recovery. Niche cultural hit, deeply memorable to the right audience. Risk: may feel too singularly gamer for broad appeal and for the gym-bro protein line.
- **Domain risk:** Low — Respawn.ca likely available. No major brand owns it.

---

**12. Epoch**
- **Tagline:** "The new era of functional fuel."
- **Rationale:** Strong, premium, speaks to a shift in the industry. French market connection (*Époque*). Two syllables, distinctive. Could feel slightly abstract for a snack — better for a platform brand than a single-product line.
- **Domain risk:** Low — Epoch.ca likely available.

---

**13. Prism**
- **Tagline:** "Refracting focus into peak performance."
- **Rationale:** Scientific, premium, visually evocative. Appeals to the "optimization" mindset in both audiences. Unique in food/beverage space. Bilingual potential. Strong visual identity potential (logo, packaging).
- **Domain risk:** Very low — Prism.ca likely available. No major brand owns it.

---

**14. Node**
- **Tagline:** "Your cognitive and physical connection point."
- **Rationale:** Network nodes (gaming infrastructure) + neural nodes (brain) + muscle insertion points (gym). Smart positioning but could feel cold/techy for an edible product. Interesting for a tech-adjacent brand but might need explaining at point of sale.
- **Domain risk:** Very low — Node.ca likely available.

---

**15. Cipher**
- **Tagline:** "Decode your potential."
- **Rationale:** Coding/gaming DNA (cipher = encryption/decoding) + brain sharpness. Premium, smart, distinctive. Strong for the nootropic line, slightly harder for protein. Cipher.ca likely available.
- **Domain risk:** Very low — Cipher.ca probably free.

---

## RECOMMENDATION

**Top pick: NeuralStack or Shift**

- **NeuralStack** — most ownable, clearest positioning, works across both product lines (nootropics + protein), strong gamer/coder identity, "stack" is native vocabulary for your audience
- **Shift** — simplest to execute, bilingual, fastest to market recognition, clean visual identity potential

**Backup: Clutch, Forge, Lucid**
