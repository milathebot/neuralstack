# LateNight — Brand Name Options
*15 options, 3 tiers, Toronto launch*

---

## TIERS USED: 3 (15 TOTAL)

---

## TIER 1 — Names I Love

---

**1. LateNight**
- **Tagline:** Stay sharp past midnight.
- **Rationale:** Time tells everything. "It's late. I'm still building." Tells time, tells mood, tells audience. Dark, moody, late-night energy. Not a wellness brand — it's a timestamp.
- **Domain:** LateNight.ca likely available. Clear.

---

**2. Sharp**
- **Tagline:** Stay sharp.
- **Rationale:** Gamer vocabulary. "Stay sharp" is intuitive, one word, super fast to brand. Could be "Sharp Focus," "Sharp Gummies," etc. Shortest path to brand recognition.
- **Domain:** Sharp.ca probably taken by Sharp Electronics. Check carefully.

---

**3. Grind**
- **Tagline:** The grind never stops.
- **Rationale:** Coder/gamer vocabulary for persistent effort. "The grind" is how they describe their work sessions. Works for late nights, 3am builds, 24-hour hackathons.
- **Domain:** Grind.ca likely available. Gritty, real.

---

## TIER 2 — Solid Options

---

**4. Shift**
- **Tagline:** The mental shift.
- **Rationale:** "Shift" is gamer vocabulary (key press) + mental shift (change in focus state). Bilingual — Shift in French reads the same. Ultra-short.
- **Domain:** Shift.ca likely available.

---

**5. Build**
- **Tagline:** Build. Ship. Repeat.
- **Rationale:** What coders do. "Build" implies creation, making, shipping. Works for the product ("build your focus") and the audience (builders). Clear positioning.
- **Domain:** Build.ca probably taken. Check.

---

**6. Stack**
- **Tagline:** Stack your focus.
- **Rationale:** Supplement vocabulary for combining nootropics + gamer vocabulary for "stacking." "Stack your focus" is a natural phrase. Implies layering benefits.
- **Domain:** Stack.ca likely available.

---

**7. NightShift**
- **Tagline:** The night shift is on.
- **Rationale:** Late-night worker energy. "Night shift" is what the audience is on — they're the ones working when everyone else sleeps. Two words, a bit more story.
- **Domain:** NightShift.ca probably available.

---

**8. Forge**
- **Tagline:** Fuel for the late-night build.
- **Rationale:** Strong, premium, works for both audiences. Evokes creation (coders building worlds) and late-night heat (forge = fire). Not time-specific though.
- **Domain:** Forge.ca likely available.

---

## TIER 3 — Wildcards

---

**9. Respawn**
- **Tagline:** Reboot. Refuel. Return.
- **Rationale:** Pure gaming DNA — respawn is the recovery mechanic. Gamers understand this deeply. Recovery = focus after a snack. Niche but memorable.
- **Domain:** Respawn.ca likely available.

---

**10. Cipher**
- **Tagline:** Decode your potential.
- **Rationale:** Coding DNA (cipher = encryption/decoding) + brain sharpness. "Cipher" is what coders understand. Premium, distinctive. Could feel cold/techy for a snack.
- **Domain:** Cipher.ca likely available.

---

**11. Node**
- **Tagline:** Your cognitive connection point.
- **Rationale:** Network nodes (gaming infrastructure) + neural nodes (brain). Smart positioning. Clean for tech/Web3 audience. Might need explaining at point of sale.
- **Domain:** Node.ca likely available.

---

**12. Clutch**
- **Tagline:** When it counts.
- **Rationale:** Gaming term for high-pressure plays + gym-adjacent. Premium-feeling, aspirational. But "clutch" might skew too gym/wellness for the builder audience.
- **Domain:** Clutch.ca likely available.

---

**13. Pulse**
- **Tagline:** Feel the pulse.
- **Rationale:** Brain pulse, energy pulse, late-night pulse. Simple, energetic, works for focus/energy. "Pulse" is also heart rate — clean energy metaphor.
- **Domain:** Pulse.ca probably taken by fitness brands.

---

**14. Epoch**
- **Tagline:** The new era of focus.
- **Rationale:** Strong, premium, speaks to a shift. French market connection (*Epoque*). Two syllables, distinctive. Could feel slightly abstract for a snack brand.
- **Domain:** Epoch.ca likely available.

---

**15. Volt**
- **Tagline:** Energy that bites back.
- **Rationale:** Electricity metaphor, fast energy, gamer-adjacent. Short, punchy. Risk: too similar to energy drink branding (Red Bull, Monster, etc.).
- **Domain:** Volt.ca probably taken.

---

## RECOMMENDATION

**Top pick: LateNight**

- Time-specific, mood-specific, audience-specific
- Owns the late-night moment completely
- "Stay sharp past midnight" is a complete positioning statement
- Dark/moody visual identity is immediately clear

**Backup: Sharp, Grind, Shift, Forge**
