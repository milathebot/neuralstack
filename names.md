# Brand Name — MunchPad
**Locked | May 2025**

---

## The Name

**MunchPad** — "Find your focus. Stay there."

A pad is where you work (your desk, your setup, your workspace). A munch is what you do while you're working. "MunchPad" is the snack that belongs on a builder's desk. It says exactly what it is, exactly who it's for, and it sounds like a brand that belongs in a coder's workflow.

---

## Tagline Options

1. **Find your focus. Stay there.** — The primary tagline. Flow-state framing, works any time of day.
2. **Snack. Stack. Ship.** — Three words, three coding verbs. Clean cadence.
3. **Pad your focus.** — Plays on workspace + padding (comfort, support). A bit more subtle.

---

## Why MunchPad Works

- **Coder-native:** A pad is your workspace. You sit at your pad. You code at your pad. It's their language.
- **Snack-native:** Munch carries the snack meaning on its own.
- **Web3-native:** Pads are in all the tools. Notion pad. Google pad. Hackpad. It's a workspace tool word.
- **Event-ready:** "Grab a MunchPad" is a real sentence you'd say to someone at a table.
- **Time-neutral:** Doesn't anchor to late-night. Works at 9am or 9pm.
