## The Name

**Overclock** — "Find your focus. Stay there."

<<<<<<< Updated upstream
A pad is where you work (your desk, your setup, your workspace). A munch is what you do while you're working. "Overclock" is the snack that belongs on a builder's desk. It says exactly what it is, exactly who it's for, and it sounds like a brand that belongs in a coder's workflow.
=======
A tool. A reference to computing. "Overclocking" a CPU means pushing it past its rated speed — getting more out of the same hardware. That's exactly what Overclock mints do: they help you get more out of your brain, same as you would with your machine.

It belongs on a builder's desk. It sounds like a brand that belongs in a coder's workflow. And it's ownable — no other snack brand has taken it.
