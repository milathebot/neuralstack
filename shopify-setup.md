## STEP 1 — Create Shopify Account

**URL:** shopify.com

**Choose:** Shopify Lite plan ($9/month) — you don't need the full POS. This gives you a buy button and a simple storefront.

1. Sign up with your email (use your real name/business email)
2. Set store name: "Overclock" or "Overclock Foods"
3. Answer the setup questions:
   - "What will you sell?" → Food and drink
   - "Are you currently selling?" → No
   - "Where do you want to start?" → Online store
4. Skip the AI store setup — I'll give you better copy
5. Complete account setup

Once your store is created, send me the admin URL. I'll prep the product page copy and design spec so you can just paste it in.

## STEP 3 — Set Up Buy Button

1. Go to **Sales Channels** → **Buy Button**
2. Click "Generate embed code"
3. Copy the generated HTML snippet
4. Send it to me — I'll embed it wherever you want (Discord, a landing page, etc.)

The buy button works on any website. We can put it on a simple link-in-bio page (Carrd, ~$19/year) so you don't need a full website.

---

## STEP 4 — Shipping Settings

1. Go to **Settings** → **Shipping and delivery**
2. Under "General shipping rates" → **Edit**
3. Add rates:
   - Canada: $5.00 flat rate (standard letter/padded mail)
   - United States: $8.00 flat rate
   - International: $12.00

For orders of 3+ tins, switch to calculated shipping (Canada Post rates).

---

## STEP 5 — Payments

1. Go to **Settings** → **Payments**
2. Shopify Payments is easiest — accepts Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay
3. For crypto: use **Coinbase Commerce** or **Shopify's crypto integration** (US only currently — if you want crypto checkout, tell me and I'll set up Coinbase Commerce separately)

---

## STEP 6 — Legal Policies (required by Shopify)

Shopify requires these before you can publish:

**Privacy policy:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Policies**
2. Shopify auto-generates a template — use it
3. Replace with: "Overclock Foods does not collect personal information beyond what is needed to fulfill orders. We do not sell or share customer data. Payment processing is handled by Shopify Payments."

**Refund policy:**
1. Use template: "All sales are final. If your order arrives damaged, contact us within 48 hours with a photo and we'll make it right."

**Terms of service:**
1. Use template: "By purchasing, you confirm you are 18+ or have parental consent. This product is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease."

## DESIGN NOTES FOR PRODUCT PHOTOS

**If you're taking photos yourself (recommended for launch):**

Tin photography:
- Surface: Dark matte surface (black foam board from Dollarama works)
- Lighting: Natural light by a window, or ring light
- Camera: Phone is fine — 1080p is enough
- Style: Clean, minimal, product-focused

Reference the aesthetic: Ledger / Arc / Linear / Raycast. Dark. Professional. Not "wellness brand." Think: dark roast coffee, not mint leaves.
