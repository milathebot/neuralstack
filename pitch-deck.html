Regulatory

Conventional food.
Same track as Tic Tac.

  • No NPN required — not a natural health product. No isolated caffeine, L-theanine, or vitamin compounds.
  • No Supplemented Foods compliance — no mandatory Front-of-Pack symbol (Jan 2026 update checked — ingredient levels below threshold).
  • No NIN (notification) needed — conventional food, not a supplement.
  • Bilingual Nutrition Facts label — standard CFIA requirement.
  • All ingredients are food ingredients — xylitol, mannitol, cocoa, coffee extract, matcha, lion's mane (culinary), vanilla, salt, stevia. No isolates requiring authorization.
Messaging rule
Describe ingredients. Never claim effects.

"focus" · "cognition" · "flow state" · "nootropic" · "brain boost" · "mental performance"
