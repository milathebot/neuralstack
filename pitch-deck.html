Risks

Honest assessment.

Regulatory
Structure/function claims need care. Wrong wording = Health Canada warning. Mitigation: budget $1,000 for professional label review before print.
Co-Manufacturer
Ontario co-packers are stretched. Lead times 4–8 weeks for first run. Mitigation: Line up 2–3 quotes before committing.
Consumer Education
Nootropic snacks are still new to mainstream. People want to know "what does this do?" Mitigation: event sampling is perfect — explain in 30 seconds.
Margin Pressure
500-unit run is expensive per unit. Scale to 2,000+ and margins improve significantly. Mitigation: Pre-sell at events to validate demand before run.

Key Regulatory Notes

  • Structure/function claims OK — no NPN needed for conventional snacks
  • No "treats/cures/prevents" language
  • Bilingual labels mandatory (EN + FR)
  • Allergen declarations required (top 10)
  • Lion's mane / reishi accepted as food ingredients
