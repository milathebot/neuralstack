## BATCH CALCULATIONS

- Batch size: 200 tins × 30 tablets = 6,000 tablets
- Each tablet: 1g
- Total blend needed: ~6kg
- Per-tablet stack:

| Ingredient | % | Grams per batch |
|-----------|---|----------------|
| Xylitol (compressible granular) | 60% | 3,600g |
| Mannitol | 10% | 600g |
| Dutch-processed cocoa powder | 8% | 480g |
| Coffee extract powder | 3% | 180g |
| Ceremonial matcha powder | 3% | 180g |
| Lion's mane dual-extract | 2% | 120g |
| Vanilla powder | 2% | 120g |
| Natural chocolate flavor powder | 1.5% | 90g |
| Sea salt | 0.5% | 30g |
| Stevia reb M | 0.3% | 18g |
| Calcium stearate | 1% | 60g |
| **Total** | **100%** | **6,000g** |

Order 10–15% extra for spillage, waste, and test batches.

## NOTES

- **Organic:** Not required but can command higher price. Consider organic xylitol and cocoa for v2.
- **All ingredients food-grade** — confirm before purchasing. Excipients (tablet-grade) not needed.
- **CFIA label requirement:** Keep ingredient receipts. Label must list all ingredients in descending order.
- **Français:** Ingredients must be listed in French on final label — source French-language COAs or accept generic French ingredient names.
